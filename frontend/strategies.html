What these are — and aren't. Every portfolio on this page is a rules-based model portfolio: hypothetical performance, paper-traded from published signals, never live money, and shown with its failures. Hypothetical results have inherent limitations — they are constructed with hindsight and do not reflect the impact of real execution, liquidity or taxes. Past performance, simulated or otherwise, does not predict future results. This is not investment advice — see the Disclaimer and Terms. More strategies will be added as they survive testing.

2. Description of service

Regime Compass is a free, publicly accessible financial data dashboard that displays market regime indicators for selected global indices. It uses statistical models — including a 3-state Gaussian Hidden Markov Model, simple moving averages, and exponential moving averages — to classify each trading day into regime states (bull, bear, neutral).

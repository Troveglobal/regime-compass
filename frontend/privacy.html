Privacy Policy

A short, plain-language statement of what Regime Compass collects and what it does with it. Last updated: today.
Short version. Regime Compass is a static, anonymous, read-only dashboard. We do not require accounts, do not store personal data, do not use tracking cookies, and do not sell anything. The only data we keep is server access logs (which any web host keeps) and basic anonymous traffic statistics.

What we collect

By default, when you visit the site, our server records standard web-server access logs containing:

  • Your IP address (truncated where possible)
  • The URL you requested and the response code
  • Timestamp of the request
  • Your browser's User-Agent string and the page you came from (Referer)

These logs are retained for up to 30 days for the purposes of debugging, security, and basic traffic analysis. They are not shared with third parties except where required by law.

If we add privacy-respecting analytics (e.g., Plausible, Fathom), it will record only aggregate, anonymous traffic metrics — never anything that identifies you personally. No cookies are set for analytics.

What we do not collect

  • No accounts. You don't sign up. You don't log in.
  • No personal data. No names, emails, addresses, phone numbers, financial information, or anything you might call "PII".
  • No tracking cookies. The site sets no cookies for advertising or tracking.
  • No third-party trackers. No Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, ad networks, etc.
  • No fingerprinting. We do not attempt to identify you across visits.
  • No data sales. We do not sell, rent, or share data with anyone.

Third-party content

The site loads a few resources from third parties to function:

  • Google Fonts — for the typefaces (Inter, Fraunces). Your browser fetches them from Google's CDN. Their privacy policy applies to that fetch.
  • jsDelivr CDN — for the Chart.js charting library. Their privacy policy applies.

We do not control these third parties. If you want to avoid them, use a browser extension that blocks third-party requests; the site will still function (charts may not render).

Cookies

Regime Compass does not set any cookies on your browser. (If you see a cookie called _csrf or similar, it is from a security middleware and is short-lived and non-tracking.)

Your rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is generally nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you are based in a jurisdiction with specific rights (GDPR, CCPA, India DPDPA), you can email the author via the LinkedIn link in the footer with any privacy-related questions or requests.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data (which would be hard given we collect none), contact the author for removal.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted at /privacy with an updated date. Continued use of the site indicates acceptance of the current policy.
